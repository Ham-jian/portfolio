People perceive many things through scents. Although scents are invisible, they have the power to shake emotions and awaken memories. Some scents bring back old memories vividly, and others make new moments special.

I believe in the power of these scents. I also want to go beyond visible design and become a designer who stays in people's emotions and memories for a long time, just like a scent that is not shaped but resonating deeply. I will try to inspire people with warm sensibilities and create designs that permeate like scents between brands and users.
